## Chmod: change permissions of a file.
- It control who can read(r), write(w) & execute(x)
- three groups of users owner, group & others

-------------------
permission | Number
-----------|-------
read	   |  4
write      |  2
execute    |  1
--------------------

```
chmod 440 <file-name>
```
- owner	   | 4	| read
- group    | 4  | read
- others   | 0  | no permission
